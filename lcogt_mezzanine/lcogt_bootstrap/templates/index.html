About Us

Las Cumbres Observatory Global Telescope network (LCOGT) is a group of scientists and engineers who have created and operate a unique global network of telescopes that functions as a single globe-spanning scientific instrument.

The LCOGT team, along with scientists and educators around the World, is using the network to understand the physics and chemistry of these phenomena, in order to answer the fundamental questions about the Universe and our place in it.
