Who are we?

Las Cumbres Observatory Global Telescope network (LCOGT) is a group of scientists and engineers who have created and operate a unique global network of telescopes that functions as a single globe-spanning scientific instrument.

With internet access, anyone in the world, at anytime, is able to make discoveries using the LCOGT global telescope network.
